### Opening of School Year 2023-2024

August 3, 2023 &mdash; The Department of Education (DepEd) announces that the
opening of classes for School Year 2023-2034 in all public schools will
be on **August 29, 2023**.

Private schools may choose to open classes on any date starting "the first
Monday of June but not later that the last day of August," pursuant to
Republic Act 11480.

Thank you.
